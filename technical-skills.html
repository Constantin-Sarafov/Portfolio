Week 1 Highlight

Trends and developments

Friday - Additionally on Friday we had a fun activity of sorts which taught us about Gestalt. Personally for me, it was quite interesting to learn about it since thanks to it I managed to implement the Figure/Ground Contrast style with the skill cards that can be seen on the homepage of this site. The reason as to why I perceive them as such is because the front and back of the card could be seen as separate "figures" against a "ground," with the hover action making the back of the card emerge as the new figure. Which creates a dynamic sense of depth or interaction, where the viewer's attention shifts between the two sides.
